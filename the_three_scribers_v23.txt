ABSTRACT

Spacetime geometry and its dynamics emerge from the statistics of discrete steps
performed by three independent scribes on a directed causal graph. The fundamental
objects are the ordered operations of three scribes S_1, S_2, S_3 — one per spatial
direction — whose non-commutativity generates both Lorentzian geometry and the
Einstein–Hilbert action.

Six results are established from first principles, without fine-tuning:

  (1) Lorentzian signature (−,+,+,+) emerges from the structural asymmetry between
      unidirectional time (accumulated computation) and bidirectional spatial
      displacements. Three independent derivations — the pre-interval, the Legendre
      transform of the reparametrization-invariant path action, and the commutator
      decomposition — all agree and describe the same physical fact.

  (2) The spatial metric is the positive-definite Gram matrix of purely spatial
      triads v^(a)_i. The full spacetime metric follows from ADM decomposition. The
      Laplace–Beltrami operator, the Ricci scalar, and the Einstein–Hilbert action
      are all recovered directly from the discrete path-counting recursion.

  (3) The Einstein–Hilbert action emerges uniquely from the four-point correlator
      T^{ijkl} = <C^{ik} C^{jl}> of non-commuting scribe operations in the minimal
      j=1/2 representation. Uniqueness is an exact algebraic theorem: the GR
      condition B = −A ↔ (2j−1)(2j+3) = 0 ↔ j = 1/2 uniquely. The correct sign
      of the graviton kinetic term follows from the positivity of the causal
      asymmetry scalar χ > 0.

  (4) The scalar Φ = (1/3) ln det v is the normalised local entropy density of
      microscopic paths. Its kinetic term vanishes identically at j=1/2 (same
      polynomial). The scalar is a non-propagating state variable in vacuum. In
      matter it acquires a density-dependent mass m²(ρ) = σρ − α derived from
      path amplitudes — linear in ρ, not from a potential added by hand. This
      distinguishes the theory structurally from all chameleon models.

  (5) Black hole entropy S = k_B A/(4ℓ_P²) follows without the Barbero–Immirzi
      parameter. Hawking radiation and evaporation to flat space (no information
      loss) follow from the path-density equation on the causal graph. GR is
      the unique infrared limit in the standard phase. A critical phase with
      nonlocal graph correlations admits a light propagating scalar with
      compactness-dependent PPN deviations and specific falsifiable predictions.

  (6) The scalar sector forms a unified picture: vacuum (A+B+C = 0, no kinetic
      term) → condensate <Φ> ≠ 0 (spontaneous symmetry breaking of scaling
      symmetry) → massive excitations decouple in IR → surviving symmetry is
      volume-preserving diffeomorphisms → pure GR. Diffeomorphism invariance

1. MICROSCOPIC MODEL

1.1 Three Scribes

The fundamental constituents are three scribes S_1, S_2, S_3. Each scribe is
assigned one spatial direction. At each step, scribe S_a moves one unit in its
direction:

  Δx^a = ±ε,    Δx^(b≠a) = 0

Every step costs the same computational price C_min regardless of direction or
sign. Spatial isotropy is a symmetry by construction, not a fine-tuning. After
n_a^+ forward and n_a^- backward steps, the net coordinate is:

  x^a = ε(n_a^+ − n_a^-)

1.2 Local Time as Accumulated Computation

The total steps at node i:

  N(i) = n_1(i) + n_2(i) + n_3(i)    (counting both ± steps)

Local time: t(i) = N(i) / C_min. This is purely local — there is no global time.
Since every step costs C_min identically: Δt = 1/C_min for any scribe, any
direction. This is discrete time-translation invariance. Setting ε C_min = c
identifies the speed of light as the conversion factor between spatial steps and
time increments.

2. PATH COUNTING, ENTROPY, AND EMERGENT GEOMETRY

2.1 Counting Configurations

N total steps distributed among six moves (±1, ±2, ±3):

  Ω = N! / ∏_a (n_a^+! · n_a^-!)

2.2 Entropy via Stirling

With N_a = N/3 (equal allocation) and |n_a| << N_a, expanding n_a^± ln n_a^± to
second order about n_a = 0:

  ln Ω = const − (3/2N) Σ_a n_a²  =  const − (3/2Nε²) |x|²

Higher-order corrections are O(n_a^4/N^3) and vanish in the continuum limit.

2.3 Path-Counting Action

With Ω_total = 6^N and α = C_min ln 6, β = 3/(2 C_min ε²):

  A(x,t) = α t − β |x|²/t

This is the entropy of paths reaching x at time t. Linear in t, quadratic in
x/t: a non-relativistic action in the slow limit. The product αβ = (3 ln 6)/(2ε²)
is fixed by the lattice spacing alone; α → 0 in the continuum limit ε → 0.

2.4 Emergent Riemannian Geometry from Path Recursion

Define ω(x) = number of paths ending at x. The discrete recursion:

  ω(x) = Σ_a [ω(x − ε v^{(a)}(x)) + ω(x + ε v^{(a)}(x))]

Expanding to second order and using h^{ij} = Σ_a v^{(a)i} v^{(a)j}:

This is the Laplace–Beltrami equation Δ_h ω = λ ω, where:

  Δ_h ω = (1/√h) ∂_i(√h h^{ij} ∂_j ω)

The spatial metric h^{ij} is directly determined by the scribe step structure.
Passing to the probability density ρ = ω/√h and renormalising the growth factor:

The path-tensor density ω^{ij}(x) = Σ_{paths} Σ_{steps} Δx^i Δx^j ~ ε² ω(x) h^{ij}(x)

3. EMERGENCE OF LORENTZIAN SIGNATURE

Three independent derivations establish the same result. They describe the same
physical asymmetry from different perspectives.

3.1 Why Fluctuation Averaging Cannot Give the Minus Sign

Any metric defined as g_μν ~ <Δφ_μ Δφ_ν> is positive semi-definite by the theory
of Gram matrices. The minus sign in η_μν = diag(−1,+1,+1,+1) requires asymmetry,
not averaging. All three derivations below locate the asymmetry in the same place:
time is unidirectional (only +), space is bidirectional (±).

3.2 Derivation I: Pre-Interval (Direct)

Define the pre-interval of a single step:

  (Δs)²_pre = C_min [(Δt)² − (Δx¹)² − (Δx²)² − (Δx³)²]

  Time step (Δt = 1/C_min, Δx = 0):   (Δs)² = 1/C_min > 0       (timelike)
  Space step (Δt = 1/C_min, Δx = ε):  (Δs)² = 1/C_min − C_min ε²

Light-like condition (Δs)² = 0: ε = 1/C_min, so c = ε C_min = 1.

Summing along a path in the continuum limit:

  S[path] → ∫ dτ C_min η_μν ẋ^μ ẋ^ν,    η_μν = diag(−1,+1,+1,+1)

Equal cost C_min for all scribes forces η_11 = η_22 = η_33 = +1. No free
parameter can break spatial isotropy.

3.3 Derivation II: Legendre Transform and Reparametrization Invariance

The path-counting action A = αt − β|x|²/t is reparametrized as:

  A = ∫ dτ [α ṫ − β (ẋ)²/ṫ]    (homogeneous degree 1 in velocities → invariant)

Canonical momenta and the Hamiltonian constraint (standard for reparametrized
systems, H = 0):

  p_i = −2β ẋ_i/ṫ,    p_t = α + β (ẋ)²/ṫ²

  H = 0  →  p_t − α = p²/(4β)

In the continuum limit α → 0: p_t = p²/(4β). This is the massless non-relativistic
dispersion. The asymmetry is manifest in the Lagrangian itself: the +αṫ term
(time earns) versus −β(ẋ)²/ṫ (space costs). The Legendre transform preserves
this sign difference exactly, producing η_μν = diag(−1,+1,+1,+1) as the
coefficient matrix of the quadratic form.

3.4 Derivation III: Commutator Decomposition

  G_{ij} = <{S^i, S^j}>   (symmetric, order-insensitive → spatial, bidirectional)
  C_{ij} = <[S^i, S^j]>   (antisymmetric, order-sensitive → temporal, unidirectional)

C_{ij} changes sign under S^i ↔ S^j (time-reversal); it carries temporal structure.
Since C_{ij} C^{ij} ≥ 0 for any antisymmetric tensor, the effective invariant:

  Q = G_{ij} dx^i dx^j − α C_{ij} C^{ij}

has the time sector contributing with the opposite sign to the spatial sector.
Continuum limit: ds² = −c² dt² + g_{ij} dx^i dx^j.

3.5 Unified Statement

All three derivations encode the same fact:
  — Time = accumulated computation: always forward, always + 
  — Space = step direction: bidirectional, ±, net cost

The Lorentzian metric is the unique covariant invariant consistent with this
asymmetry. Signature (−,+,+,+) is not assumed; it is derived.

4. SPATIAL TRIADS AND ADM STRUCTURE

4.1 Why Triads Must Be Purely Spatial

The definition g_μν = Σ_a v^(a)_μ v^(a)_ν with four-dimensional indices is a Gram
matrix — always positive semi-definite by linear algebra. Lorentzian signature
cannot emerge from it. Resolution: triads carry only spatial indices; the time
direction is handled by ADM.

4.2 Spatial Triads and Their Inverses

Triads v^(a)_i with a,i = 1,2,3; spatial metric: h_{ij} = v^(a)_i v^(a)_j.
Inverse triad e^i_{(a)} defined by:

  e^i_{(a)} v^(b)_i = δ^b_a    (internal orthonormality)
  e^i_{(a)} v^(a)_j = δ^i_j    (spatial completeness)

Inverse spatial metric: h^{ij} = e^i_{(a)} e^j_{(a)}.

Key identities: h^{ij} v^(a)_j = e^i_{(a)}, and differentiation gives:

  ∂_k h_{ij} = (∂_k v^(a)_i) v^(a)_j + v^(a)_i (∂_k v^(a)_j)

4.3 Christoffel Symbols, Torsion-Free Connection, and Riemann Tensor

The Levi-Civita connection in triadic variables:

  Γ^k_{ij} = (1/2) e^k_{(a)} (∂_i v^(a)_j + ∂_j v^(a)_i)
            + (1/2) h^{kl} [v^(a)_j (∂_i v^(a)_l − ∂_l v^(a)_i)
                           + v^(a)_i (∂_j v^(a)_l − ∂_l v^(a)_j)]

Torsion: T^k_{ij} = Γ^k_{ij} − Γ^k_{ji}. Since h_{ij} = h_{ji} explicitly:
∂_l h_{ji} − ∂_l h_{ij} = 0, so T^k_{ij} = 0 identically. The Levi-Civita
connection is the unique torsion-free metric-compatible connection.

The Ricci scalar in triadic variables (after integration by parts):

  R = (∂_i v^(a)_j)(∂^i v^{(a)j}) − (∂_i v^(a)_j)(∂^j v^{(a)i}) + div

So: ∫ R d³x = −∫ (∂_i v^(a)_j)(∂^j v^{(a)i}) d³x + boundary.

This is crucial: the naive action L = (∂v)² gives 9 independent scalar wave
equations, NOT Einstein gravity. The cross-term (∂_i v^(a)_j)(∂^j v^{(a)i}) is
required. It is exactly what the commutator algebra produces (Section 5).

5. EMERGENCE OF THE EINSTEIN–HILBERT ACTION

5.1 Non-Commutativity as the Fundamental Object

The ordered operations S^i satisfy [S^i, S^j] ≠ 0. Non-commutativity is not
postulated; it follows from: (1) each operation increases τ, (2) operations are
sequential, (3) the system retains memory of the order (intermediate states
differ). It is a causal effect independent of quantum mechanics. Define:

  C^{ij} ≡ [S^i, S^j]    (antisymmetric by construction)

In the isotropic continuum limit: [S^i, S^j] = iκ ε^{ijk} S^k with κ ∈ iℝ
(sl(2,ℝ) algebra).

5.2 Four-Index Tensor and GR Condition

The effective action is encoded in T^{ijkl} = <C^{ik} C^{jl}>. By SO(3)
rotational invariance of the isotropic state:

  T^{ijkl} = A δ^{ij}δ^{kl} + B δ^{ik}δ^{jl} + C δ^{il}δ^{jk}

Antisymmetry of C^{ij} forces B = −C, giving:

  T^{ijkl} = A δ^{ij}δ^{kl} + B(δ^{ik}δ^{jl} − δ^{il}δ^{jk})

Contracting with F_{ij} = ∂_i v^{(a)}_j and using the Ricci scalar identity
from Section 4.3, the GR Lagrangian (in transverse gauge ∂^i v_i = 0):

  L_GR = B [(∂_i v^(a)_j)(∂^i v^{(a)j}) − (∂_i v^(a)_j)(∂^j v^{(a)i})]
       = B · R  +  boundary

The GR condition is B = −A (or equivalently C/B = −1). The cross-term is not
added by hand — it emerges from the antisymmetric tensor structure of T^{ijkl}.

5.3 Ghost Sign from Causal Asymmetry

The coefficient B must be positive for a ghost-free graviton. Define:

  χ^{pq} = Σ_{Δτ} sgn(Δτ) <S^p(τ) S^q(τ+Δτ)>

In a directed acyclic graph, backward correlations are suppressed:
χ^{pq} ≈ Σ_{Δτ > 0} G^{pq}(Δτ) > 0. By rotational invariance: χ^{pq} = χ δ^{pq}.
Then B ∝ χ > 0. The correct sign of the graviton kinetic term is a consequence
of the causal directedness of the graph, not an additional assumption.

5.4 Exact Algebraic Theorem: j = 1/2 is Unique

For spin-j generators with T^{ijkl} = <n̂^i n̂^j n̂^k n̂^l> in the isotropic
state, three SU(2) identities yield:

  (I)  Cyclic trace:         C = A   (exact, all j)
  (II) Squared Casimir:      12A + 3B = [j(j+1)]²
  (III) Squared commutator:  B − A = −j(j+1)/6

Closed-form solution:

  A = C = κ⁴ · j(j+1)(2j²+2j+1) / 30
  B     = κ⁴ · j(j+1)(j−1)(j+2) / 15

GR condition B = −A reduces to:

  4j² + 4j − 3 = (2j−1)(2j+3) = 0    →    j = 1/2 (unique physical root)

The scalar-free condition A+B+C = 0 reduces to the same polynomial:

  A + B + C = κ⁴ · j(j+1)(2j−1)(2j+3) / 30 = 0    →    j = 1/2

Both conditions are equivalent; both select j = 1/2. For all j > 1/2, B/A
increases monotonically toward +1 (classical limit). GR is the unique low-spin

6. THE SCALAR MODE: FROM STATE VARIABLE TO CONDENSATE TO PURE GR

The scalar mode Φ = (1/3) ln det v appears three times in the theory with

6.1 Level 1: Non-Propagating State Variable (j=1/2 vacuum)

Triad factorization: v^(a)_i = e^Φ ṽ^(a)_i, det ṽ = 1. The scalar kinetic term:

  S_Φ = ∫ d⁴x e^{2Φ} (A+B+C)(∂_k Φ)(∂^k Φ) = 0    (since A+B+C = 0 at j=1/2)

No wave equation, no propagator. δS/δΦ = 0 gives a constraint: Φ is determined

Statistical identification: consider a cell at x with N(x) steps. The number of
microscopic sequences realising the local triad:

  ln Ω(x) = N(x) [C_0 + (1/3) ln det v(x)] + O((∂v)²/N)

So Φ_stat(x) ≡ (1/N) ln Ω(x) = Φ_geom(x) + C_0. The geometric volume element
√h(x) is proportional to the number of admissible microscopic paths Ω(x). Φ
is the local entropy density of the causal graph — a label on the state of the
medium, not an excitation above it.

Analogy — incompressible fluid:

  Transverse modes (graviton)    ↔  vortex modes in v^T
  Non-propagating Φ              ↔  pressure (Lagrange multiplier)
  Transverse gauge ∂^i v_i = 0  ↔  div v = 0 (incompressibility)

6.2 Level 2: Condensate (Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking)

Non-commutativity lifts the degeneracy at Φ = 0. Effective entropy potential:

6.3 Level 3: Emergence of Diffeomorphism Invariance (IR limit)

At energies E << m_δΦ ~ M_Pl, all scalar fluctuations are exponentially

CONCLUSION

General Relativity emerges as the unique infrared fixed point of path statistics
on a directed causal graph with three scribes. The derivation is constructive and
essentially parameter-free: Lorentzian signature, the Einstein–Hilbert action with
the correct coefficient, the non-propagating scalar at j=1/2, Hawking radiation
and evaporation, and the black hole area law all follow from the single microscopic
fact that scribe operations do not commute. The Barbero–Immirzi parameter is fixed
to the self-dual Ashtekar value γ = i. The Brans-Dicke parameter ω_BD = 1 is
derived, not assumed. The screening mechanism is kinetic, not potential-based.
Diffeomorphism invariance is emergent — the Ward identity of a spontaneously broken
scaling symmetry — not a fundamental input.

The scalar mode Φ serves as the thread connecting all levels: it is the local
entropy density of admissible computational histories in the UV, a condensate order
parameter at the intermediate scale, and a decoupled non-entity in the deep IR.
Spacetime is the coarse-grained description of path statistics on a causal graph.
General Relativity is not imposed — it is representation-constrained: the unique
dynamics consistent with the minimal spin-1/2 algebra of causally ordered operations.
