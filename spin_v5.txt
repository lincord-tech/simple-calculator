CONCLUSION

Ghost-free Einstein-Hilbert structure arises from three causally ordered
operators on a future-directed DAG via the full time-ordered correlator T_TO.
Causal ordering converts ghost gauge dynamics (isotropic: B=0, C=-A<0,
S<0) into ghost-free gravitational dynamics (causal: B_TO>0, C_TO=-B_TO,
S>0) by two simultaneous effects:

(1) Index transposition: epsilon^{ikm}epsilon^{jlm} (E1, Yang-Mills) ->
    epsilon^{ijm}epsilon^{klm} (E2, EH), implemented by the temporal commutator.

(2) Sign flip: A<0 (ghost) -> B_TO>0 (ghost-free), enforced by chi>0
    (future-directedness of the DAG).

The isotropic 2-point gives ghost Yang-Mills, not zero (v4 error corrected).
The symmetrized 4-point gives zero dynamics (B=C). Only the causal time-ordered
object gives physical, ghost-free gravitational dynamics.

Five results remain confirmed unconditionally: gamma=i, B_TO>0 from chi>0,
ghost-free sign from future-directedness, Minimality Theorem (conditional),
M_Pl^2 formula. The central equation (2j-1)(2j+3)=0 stands as a minimality
condition. Its derivation from the causal mechanism — closing the logical chain
causal ordering => B=-A => j=1/2 — is the main open question.
