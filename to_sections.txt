### 1. Новый Abstract (замени старый полностью)

Abstract  
We construct a model in which spacetime geometry emerges from the statistics of discrete steps performed by three independent scribes on a directed causal graph. The fundamental degrees of freedom are the scribe operations, whose causal ordering and non-commutativity generate both the geometry and its dynamics.

We demonstrate that:  
(1) Lorentzian signature (−,+,+,+) arises directly from the asymmetry between unidirectional time (accumulated computation) and bidirectional spatial displacements.  
(2) The spatial metric is the positive-definite Gram matrix of purely spatial triads, and the full spacetime metric is recovered via the ADM decomposition, with lapse and shift determined by the causal graph.  
(3) The Einstein–Hilbert action emerges uniquely from the four-point correlator of non-commuting scribe operations in the minimal j=1/2 representation.  
(4) The scalar Φ = (1/3) ln det v is re-identified as the normalized local entropy density of microscopic paths, Φ(x) ≈ (1/N(x)) ln Ω(x). Due to the algebraic identity A + B + C = 0 at j = 1/2, its kinetic term vanishes identically, rendering Φ a non-propagating statistical state variable of the computational medium rather than a dynamical field.  
(5) The background value ⟨Φ⟩(t) evolves slowly under the entropic potential generated by commutator-induced degeneracy lifting. This evolution produces a running of the effective gravitational constant that lies far below current observational bounds in all allowed regimes. The condensate of Φ contributes a Planck-scale cosmological constant whose smallness requires fine-tuning. No propagating massless scalar, long-range fifth force, or extra radiation-like component appears in the infrared.  
(6) Black hole entropy follows the area law S = A/4ℓ_P² without the Barbero–Immirzi parameter.

General Relativity therefore emerges as the unique infrared limit of the theory, with the statistical volume mode Φ encoding the local density of admissible computational histories. Spacetime is the coarse-grained description of path statistics on a causal graph.

### 2. Новый Conclusion (замени старый полностью)

Conclusion

We have constructed a framework in which spacetime and General Relativity emerge from the statistics of discrete steps performed by three scribes on a directed causal graph. The key results are as follows.

Lorentzian signature emerges from the fundamental causal asymmetry between time, defined as accumulated computation, and space, defined by bidirectional displacements. The spatial metric is induced as the positive-definite Gram matrix of spatial triads, and the full spacetime metric is assembled via the ADM decomposition.

At the level of the effective action, the Einstein–Hilbert term is obtained uniquely in the minimal j=1/2 representation of the non-commuting scribe operations. The same algebraic condition A + B + C = 0 that selects the correct tensor structure for gravity simultaneously causes the kinetic term of the scalar mode Φ = (1/3) ln det v to vanish identically.

By identifying Φ with the normalized local entropy density of microscopic paths, Φ(x) ≈ (1/N(x)) ln Ω(x), the geometric and statistical definitions coincide in the large-N limit. Consequently, Φ is a non-propagating state variable rather than a dynamical field. Its background value ⟨Φ⟩(t) evolves slowly under an entropic potential generated by commutator-induced degeneracy lifting. This evolution induces a running of the effective gravitational constant G_eff that is many orders of magnitude below current experimental bounds in all physically allowed regimes. The vacuum energy of the Φ condensate contributes a Planck-scale cosmological constant, whose reduction to the observed value requires an extreme cancellation — an entropic reformulation of the cosmological constant problem.

As a result, the infrared theory is pure Einstein gravity without additional propagating scalars or long-range fifth forces. Black hole entropy follows the standard area law without requiring a tunable Barbero–Immirzi parameter. Cosmological expansion is governed by ordinary matter, with only Planck-suppressed corrections from the statistical volume mode.

The conceptual picture is economical and unifying: geometry is the coarse-grained statistics of computational paths, the metric encodes the local multiplicity of microscopic histories consistent with the causal structure, and gravity arises as the effective dynamics that maximises the number of such realisations. General Relativity is not imposed by hand but appears as the natural infrared fixed point of the underlying path-counting theory.

Several open questions remain, most notably the precise microscopic derivation of the coefficients in the entropic potential, the detailed coarse-graining procedure from the j=1/2 algebra to the effective tensor sector, and whether a natural mechanism exists for suppressing the condensate contribution to the cosmological constant. Nevertheless, the present construction provides a concrete, internally consistent route from discrete causal processes to emergent classical spacetime, with the volume mode Φ serving as a statistical label on the computational content of geometry.

9.6 Cosmological and Phenomenological Implications of the Statistical Volume Mode

The identification Φ(x) ≈ (1/N(x)) ln Ω(x) has direct consequences for cosmology and phenomenology. Because the kinetic term for Φ vanishes identically at j = 1/2, Φ is a non-propagating state variable. Its background value ⟨Φ⟩(t) can nevertheless evolve slowly, driven by the entropic potential

V_eff(Φ) = −|α| Φ² + β Φ⁴ + O(Φ⁶),

where |α| > 0 originates from commutator-induced degeneracy lifting and β > 0 from large-deviation suppression at strong triad distortions.

9.6.1 Background evolution and running G

In a Friedmann–Robertson–Walker background the overdamped equation for the volume mode is

3H d⟨Φ⟩/dt + dV_eff/dΦ ≈ 0.

The coefficient |α| scales as |α| ∼ κ² N^γ with 0 ≤ γ ≤ 2. Since N(t) ∝ a³(t), the vacuum value v = √(|α|/(2β)) evolves slowly. The effective gravitational constant satisfies

G_eff(t) ∝ exp(−3 ⟨Φ(t)⟩),

so

d ln G_eff / dt = −3 d⟨Φ⟩/dt.

Explicit evaluation for the representative regimes yields:

- γ = 0 (local limit from ⟨Φ(x)Φ(0)⟩ calculation): |d ln G/dt| ≲ 10^{-50} yr^{-1} (Planck-suppressed).  
- γ = 1 (weak correlations): |d ln G/dt| ∼ 10^{-30}–10^{-40} yr^{-1}.  
- γ = 2 (strong correlations): |d ln G/dt| ∼ 10^{-10} yr^{-1} (already excluded by lunar laser ranging).

Current bounds |dG/G dt| ≲ (2–4)×10^{-13} yr^{-1} are satisfied only for γ ≤ 1. In all physically allowed regimes the running of G is far below detectability.

9.6.2 Condensate contribution to the cosmological constant

The vacuum energy of the condensate at v = √(|α|/(2β)) is V(v) = −|α|²/(4β). This contributes a positive effective cosmological constant

Λ_eff ∼ |α|² / (4β).

For γ = 0, Λ_eff is of order the Planck scale. For γ > 0 it is even larger. Matching the observed value Λ_obs ≈ 10^{-120} M_Pl⁴ therefore requires an extreme cancellation between the entropic condensate contribution and other vacuum-energy terms generated during coarse-graining. This reframes the cosmological constant problem as an entropic fine-tuning issue within the path-counting framework.

9.6.3 Absence of a critical phase

Explicit computation of ⟨Φ(x) Φ(0)⟩ from the generalised Stirling expansion plus commutator corrections shows short-range correlations (σ ≪ 1, η ≳ 1). The condition for a light propagating scalar is therefore not satisfied in the standard phase. No parametrically light scalar or observable fifth force emerges from the minimal construction.
