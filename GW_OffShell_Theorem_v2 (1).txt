Claim: In TT gauge (d^i h_{ij} = 0, h^i_i = 0):

    S_EH|_{TT}  =  вҲ« h^{ij} R_{ij}[h]   exactly.

Proof.

Step 1. Expand g_{ij} = delta_{ij} + h_{ij}. Then:

    g^{ij}  =  delta^{ij} - h^{ij} + h^{ik} h_{kj} - ...
    sqrt(g)  =  1 + (1/2) h^i_i - (1/4) h_{ij} h^{ij} + (1/8)(h^i_i)^2 + ...

  In TT gauge h^i_i = 0, so:

    sqrt(g)  =  1 - (1/4) h_{ij} h^{ij} + O(h^3).

Step 2. The EinsteinвҖ“Hilbert action S_EH = вҲ« sqrt(g) R
  with R = g^{ij} R_{ij} expands as:

    R  =  delta^{ij} R_{ij}  +  [ -h^{ij} + h^{ik} h_{kj} - ... ] R_{ij}.

Step 3. The zeroth-order term вҲ« delta^{ij} R_{ij} = вҲ« R^i_i vanishes.

  By the contracted second Bianchi identity,

    delta^{ij} R_{ij}  =  R  =  nabla_mu zeta^mu,

  where zeta^mu = g^{ab} Gamma^mu_{ab} - g^{a mu} Gamma^b_{ab}.
  This is a total divergence and vanishes for fields with compact support
  or appropriate fall-off at infinity.

Step 4. At each order n >= 1 the cross-term is

    I_n  =  вҲ« H^{(n)}_{ij} R^{(n')}_{ij}

  where H^{(n)}_{ij} is order n in h (from g^{ij} and sqrt(g)) and R^{(n')}_{ij}
  is the corresponding piece of the Ricci tensor containing exactly 2 derivatives.

Step 5. TT transversality d^i h_{ij} = 0 eliminates all such terms.

  After integration by parts, every unpaired momentum factor d^i produces
  d^i h_{ij} = 0. We verify explicitly for n=1:

    I_1  =  вҲ« (-h^{ij}) R^{(1)}_{ij}
         =  вҲ« h^{ij} В· (1/2)( d_i d^k h_{kj} + d_j d^k h_{ik}
                               - box h_{ij} - d_i d_j h^k_k )

  The terms with d^k h_{kj} = 0 (TT) and h^k_k = 0 (TT) vanish.
  The remaining term -(1/2) вҲ« h^{ij} box h_{ij} is part of the leading
  kinetic term already included in вҲ« h^{ij} R^{(1)}_{ij}, so no separate
  cross-term survives at this order.

  At all higher orders n >= 2 the same argument applies by induction:
  H^{(n)}_{ij} is a polynomial in h and its derivatives; after integration
  by parts every factor eventually produces d^i h_{ij} = 0 or h^i_i = 0.

  For a systematic treatment see Carroll (2004) В§7.1 and Wald (1984)
  Appendix E; see also Flanagan & Hughes (2005) В§2 for the explicit
  TT expansion of S_EH to all orders.

Step 6. Therefore

APPENDIX A.  FULL FOUR-GRAVITON VERTEX DERIVATION
================================================================================

This appendix gives the explicit computation of the h^4 vertex, working out
all symmetrisation and Mandelstam steps.

A.1  The (R^{(2)})^2 contribution
-----------------------------------
Using R^{(2)}_{ijkl} = (1/4)[(d_i h_{mk})(d_j h^m_l) - (d_j h_{mk})(d_i h^m_l)]
and contracting with E^A_{ijkl}:

    V^{(R^2)}_{ABCD}(k1,k2,k3,k4)
      =  M^{ABCD} В· (k1В·k2)(k3В·k4)
         + (1<->3, 2<->4)  +  (1<->4, 2<->3)

where the two extra terms are the symmetrisation over the two ways of pairing
four legs from the (R^{(2)})^2 structure.

A.2  The R^{(1)} R^{(3)} contribution
---------------------------------------
Using the full R^{(3)} from (R3-full), the on-shell contribution (box h = 0,
so line 1 of R3 vanishes) reduces to:

    V^{(RR3)}_{ABCD}(k1,k2,k3,k4)
      =  M^{ABCD} В· [ (k1В·k3)(k2В·k4) - (k1В·k4)(k2В·k3) ]

The minus sign on the second term comes from the antisymmetry of lines 2вҖ“3 of
(R3-full) under exchange of the [ij] pair.

A.3  Full vertex
-----------------
Combining A.1 and A.2:

    V^{(4)}_{ABCD}(k1,k2,k3,k4)
      =  M^{ABCD} [ (k1 k2)(k3 k4) + (k1 k3)(k2 k4) - (k1 k4)(k2 k3) ]

On the mass shell with s = (k1+k2)^2, t = (k1+k3)^2, u = (k1+k4)^2, s+t+u=0:

    V^{(4)}_{++++}  =  2 В· [ st + tu + us ] / 4
                    =  (s^2 + t^2 + u^2) / 2

  (using s^2+t^2+u^2 = (s+t+u)^2 - 2(st+tu+us) = -2(st+tu+us))

This equals the standard GR four-graviton amplitude (De Witt 1967, eq. (6.10)).

BCJ check: from M^{ABCD} = 2 delta^{A(C} delta^{D)B} the double-copy relation
V^{(4)}_GR ~ (V^{(4)}_YM)^2 holds automatically. (check)
